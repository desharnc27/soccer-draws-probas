Important note: this is only a demo version, which has very limited features.
The only path of options available is: [any data folder] -> exact -> load. Consequently:
- You can't choose "simulator" instead of "exact"
- You can't choose "restart" instead of "load"
Otherwise the program will experience a failure and close. 
Concretely, the purpose of this is to give you access to the qatar2022 probabilities , but not to the algorithm behind these probabilities.
